The Philippines is a wonderful country which offers many tourist attractions, beautiful culture, and welcoming people!
Here are some of the popular tourist attractions and destinations.

Cebu

Cebu is a province in Central Visayas which is known to be one of the best places for tourist attractions and exciting swimming experiences.

Baguio

Baguio, or "City of Pines", is a moutainous town in Luzon which is known for its cool climate and pine forests.

El Nido

El Nido is a municipality in Palawan island which is known for its white sand beaches, coral reefs, and amazing mountains.

Chocolate Hills

Chocolate Hills is a mountainous formation in Bohol which is known for having color-changing hills in dry season and beautiful nature.

Boracay

Boracay is a small island in Western Visayas and it is known for its clear waters, white sand beaches, and beautiful sunsets.

Tagaytay

Tagaytay is a town in the south of Manila and it is known for the eye-taking scenery, which also includes an amazing view of Taal Volcano.
